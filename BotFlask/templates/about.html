What is AryaBot?

AryaBot is a web app that can be used for purchasing limited and sought after sneakers and clothing. Nowadays the resell market is very vast and many people make a killing off of purchasing these sought after items and selling them for a marked up price, often for 3-4x the original asking price of the product. Speed is very important in these type of scenarios as many have bots and are racing to buy a product. Going manual is not the preferred way to purchase products anymore. Many sites have implemented anit-bot measures but the bot community puts in a lot of effort to surpass those and keep success high. AryaBot is my first take at trying to create my own bot. It is a very simple and dumbed down version of what a full fledged bot is. AryaBot isn't the fastest bot in the market by any means but it still gives you an edge over users that try to purchase a product manually. I hope with more experience and research I can keep on adding features to make AryaBot a serious contendor in the market!
